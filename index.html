HOW IT WORKS

The creator stays in control

  1. 1

    Connect an account

    Authorize CrossPost Studio through the platform’s official sign-in and consent screen.

  2. 2

    Select an original local video

    Choose a video you own or are authorized to publish. CrossPost Studio does not scrape or download videos from social networks.

  3. 3

    Review every post

    Edit metadata and manually choose the settings supported by each destination and account.

  4. 4

    Confirm and publish

    Nothing is posted automatically. Uploading begins only after the creator selects destinations and presses Publish.

PRIVACY BY DESIGN

Local app, direct platform connections

CrossPost Studio does not operate a public media library. Connected-account tokens are encrypted using Windows protection, and selected videos are transferred only to the platforms chosen by the creator.

FAQ

Common questions

Does CrossPost Studio publish without permission?

No. Each destination must be connected through its official authorization process, selected for the current video and confirmed by the creator.

Can each account use different text?

Yes. Creators can keep shared starting details or customize supported metadata for a specific connected account.

How does TikTok publishing work?

CrossPost Studio uses TikTok’s official Login Kit and Content Posting API. TikTok controls which publishing options are available to each creator and application.

Where can I request help or data deletion?

Use the public Support page for troubleshooting, privacy requests and account-disconnection instructions.
