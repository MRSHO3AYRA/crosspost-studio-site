CrossPost Studio Support

Connection help, privacy requests and account deletion

Request support

Report a connection or publishing problem through the project’s public support tracker. Never include passwords, client secrets, access tokens or private video files in a public report.

Open support tracker

Disconnect an account

Open Connected Accounts in CrossPost Studio, locate the account and choose the remove button. You can also revoke CrossPost Studio in the connected-app settings of the relevant platform.

Delete local application data

Remove connected accounts first, then uninstall CrossPost Studio. You may also clear the application data from your Windows user profile. Platform posts are controlled separately by the destination platform.

Privacy request

Use the support tracker and label the request “Privacy.” Do not post sensitive information publicly. Request a private follow-up channel in the issue without including private identifiers.

Read Privacy Policy

Publishing troubleshooting

Reconnect the affected account

If authorization expires or a permission changes, remove and reconnect the affected account through the official platform sign-in.

Check the selected video

Confirm that the video uses a format, duration and size supported by the destination platform.

Review platform approval and limits

Public publishing availability can depend on platform API review, account eligibility, daily quotas and the creator’s current privacy settings.

Security reminder: CrossPost Studio support will never ask you to publish or send a Client Secret, password, OAuth access token or recovery code.
